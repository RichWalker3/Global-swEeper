Returns & Exchanges Policy

Return Window

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.

Exchanges are free and can be made within 45 days of purchase.

Return Fees

Returns via Happy Returns are $10 per label.

UPS mailback returns cost $15, deducted from your refund.

Final Sale Items

The following items are final sale and cannot be returned:

  • Underwear and intimates
  • Swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Gift cards
  • Items marked "Final Sale"

How to Start a Return

Visit our returns portal at Loop Returns to initiate your return.

Price Adjustments

Price adjustments are available within 7 days of purchase if an item goes on sale.
